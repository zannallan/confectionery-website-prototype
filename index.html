About Us

While maintaining the original recipes of the most iconic pieces, today our master chocolatier, Coco Casava, leads our inspiration for new products. Fully certified with over 30 years of experience, Coco has combined with her natural curiosity and artistic creativity, challenges our concepts of what can be created with chocolate. Constantly researching new techniques, testing new recipes, and combining unique flavours, Coco strives for perfection in all things chocolate, and her eager taste testing team is always happy to help!
